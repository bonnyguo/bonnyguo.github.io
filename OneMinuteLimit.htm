OneMinuteLimit Bookmarklet

This bookmarklet forces you to read single web page in one minute.

Details

search links

Digital media changed the way we get information. We can open and view the website whenever and wherever we want. We don't value or respect the information anymore. Sometimes, a few pages are opened and then forgoten. This bookmarklet inspires people to rethink about digtial media and forces people to view the page with a time limit.

On the other side, some of the digital media keeps feeding us useless information. People develop addiction and regret after wasting hours. This bookmarklet also puts a time limit so that people won't waste so much time browsing meanless informations.
